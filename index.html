About Me

Hi, I'm Maggie! I'm an undergraduate studying Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, and Physics at MIT. Research

Currently, I'm working in Professor Marin Soljacic's lab at MIT, creating an "AI Scientist" powered by an LLM with RAG to automate the research process, specifically in photonics. I'm focusing on novel idea generation – getting the agent to generate innovate and feasible research ideas on par with those ideated by experts in the field. My other ongoing research project is in photonic crystals; we designed a 3d photonic crystal that can confine light without a bandgap, as a bound state in the continuum.
