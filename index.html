About Me

I'm Maggie, a second year undergraduate studying EECS and Physics at MIT. I'm generally interested in efficient ML and computer architecture.

Research

Currently, I'm working on a project on adaptive temperature scaling for constant-entropy LLM token generation.

I've been working in the Photonics and Modern Electromagnetics Group under Professor Marin Soljacic at MIT since September 2023. My ongoing research project that we are writing up for publication is in photonic crystals; we designed a 3d photonic crystal that can confine light without a bandgap, instead with a bound state in the continuum. You can find me at CLEO 2025 giving a presentation on our project!

As a final research project for my Deep Learning class at MIT, I worked with my classmate to explore the relationships between the query, key, and value matrices in the attention mechanism of transformers. Click here to read our blog post "A Study of Variation Across Attention Heads and Layers".
